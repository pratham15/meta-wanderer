Meta-wanderer girls

NFT COLLECTION

This collection is designed by a 16y/o Indian artist - Vidushi, and her brother.

This project features strong and independent women, who are wandering around, exploring the metaverse, going to different places, styling different outfits, and having fun!

WE AIM TO

  • Build a close-knit community of women, in the metaverse, who have a similar vision
  • Work towards the betterment of all women, and help them
  • Donate 10% of all my profits to non-profits and charities towards child-education
  • Encourage and inspire young artists
  • Create a safe space, for all. A community which helps each other, loves each other, and most importantly, listens to each other
